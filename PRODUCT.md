# Motivate Me

## register
brand

## Product Purpose
Motivate Me is a mobile app that delivers personalized motivational phrases based on a short questionnaire about the user's current mood, focus area, and preferred support style. The web landing page serves as the entry point: users answer 3 quick questions, then download the app or log in. The phrases are tailored — not generic affirmations.

## Users
People who feel drained, stuck, anxious, or just need a daily push. Not productivity optimizers or life-hack enthusiasts — people who need a friend's voice in their pocket. Casual, self-aware, probably 20s–30s. They've tried journaling apps and don't finish them.

## Brand Voice
Casual, warm, direct. Like a supportive friend who gets it — not a life coach, not a therapist. Zero toxic positivity. The copy should feel like a text, not a campaign. Examples from the product: "No worries tho I have a solution", "Take this small questionnaire, to let the support bro leaving some motivation."

## Anti-references
- Headspace / Calm (too zen, too polished, too expensive-feeling)
- Generic SaaS landing pages with feature-benefit grids
- Neon-on-black hustle culture
- Sky blue and white wellness branding
- Pastel illustration characters
